How can your school's students sign up for the competition?

Participating in Srijanas competitions is a great way for students to showcase their creativity and artistic skills. We welcome all schools in India to participate in our national-level competitions and allow their students to shine.

Signing up for the competition is easy! Here's how your school's students can participate:

Invitation and announcement:

Once your school receives an invitation from us, it should be announced to all the students by the art teacher. The students can then choose to participate in one or all competitions based on their preferences.

Registration and fees:

The art teacher needs to collect the respective fees of Rs. 50/- per competition from the students who wish to participate. The photocopy of the respective competition is to be provided to the student after the collection of fees.

Submission of entries

The students need to work on their art pieces and submit them to the art teacher. The art teacher will then select the best entries from the school and send them to us via post or courier.

Payment:

Once the entries are submitted, 15% of the total amount collected by the school is to be utilized for taking photocopies and sending the papers to us. The remaining 85% amount is to be sent to us either through a D.D. (Demand Draft) or directly transfer the amount by NEFT to the organization's account. Schools can also choose to pay the amount through the gateway by visiting our website.

Notifications:

After the entries are received and evaluated by our panel of judges, the winners will be informed by personal letter through the schools. The proud winners of the national competition and their parents will be informed, and we are confident that schools too will be proud to have students graduating from national to international competitions.

We encourage schools to participate in our competitions and give their students a chance to explore their creativity and artistic skills. It is a great opportunity for students to showcase their talent and compete with other students from across the country.

If you have any queries about the registration process or need any assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us.

We are always happy to help and answer any questions you may have. So, what are you waiting for?

Sign up your school's students for Srijanas competitions today and give them the chance to showcase their artistic skills to the world!
